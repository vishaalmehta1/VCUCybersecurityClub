About Us

The Cyber Security Club @ VCU student organization's primary goals are to create greater student involvement in information security programs, to provide greater opportunities for learning and experience to students in the information security field, and to create a joint force between students, faculty, and staff to help defend VCU from outside attacks in the form of exercises, training, and participation in cybersecurity programs held at VCU.

Please tell your friends and invite others to our club. Thank you!
